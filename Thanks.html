Thank You

Thank you for taking your time to read my story. I hope that this stays with you and prompts you to take action. Daily, there are millions of girls, and boys, who are mistreated. And we can't keep turn a blind eye towards this and hope that it just goes away, because it won't. We need to take action, together, to stop the gender inequalities and make a better world, a better tomorrow.

Remember that heroes do what's right, and not what's easy. Will you be a hero?
