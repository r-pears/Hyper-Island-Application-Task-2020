You managed to get out of the country fairly easily. The backpack with supplies that David gave you really saved your life during those times. You have no idea if your father ever found out that David helped you escape your fate, but you hope not. You wish every day that your mother, sisters, Susan, and David are fine, that your father hasn't retaliated against your loved ones.

You haven't decided what to do now. Your money, and the money David gave you, are all out. You spent it all on the bus tickets out from the country. You don't have any education, you don't know how to read or write. There is probably not any chance for you to move to a Western country, and go to school, but maybe you could get there and get a job. If you move to a Western country, you might be able to get a decent life. However, you are aware that there are racism and segregation. You don't know if you want to face that on your own, without any friends or family, without any support. But that raises another question, how are you going to support yourself if you stay here? Almost all regions of Africa is dominated by males, which is one reason that you left. Women aren't allowed to work, with very few exceptions, aren't allowed to own property. How are you going to survive if you stay on the continent?

It is starting to get dark, and you haven't found any 'safe' place to spend the night at. You head down an alley to your left, immediately you notice that something is strange. You eye the surroundings warily, you see the shadows of figures further down, but you can't see them clearly.

You carefully step forward. Suddenly, the smell hits you - a mix of urine, sweat, and something rotten. You almost gag from the smell, you bring up your arm to cover your mouth with your sleeve.
