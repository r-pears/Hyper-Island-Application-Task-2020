Your Firstborn

You're standing outside your bedroom. Your wife is inside giving birth to your first baby, she is aided by her mother and her aunt. You pace to the other side of the room. You hear your wife scream from the other side of the door, and you rush up to the door, putting your hands on the door. Your heart is beating so hard, your blood is pulsing in your ears. You take a deep breath and start pacing again. Your head is swirling, you can't believe that this is happening. It's a little over a year since you married Neela, as all marriages it has had it's ups and downs, but you're happy. Your father wanted you to marry his friend's daughter, and take her as your second wife, but you refused. You're working as an electrical engineer in the city, and Neela became a housewife. She wanted to work, but her father told her she couldn't marry and work. She chose to marry you, you've always felt some resentment from her, in regards to giving up the only independence she had.

"Congratulations."

You snap out of your musings and look up at your mother-in-law, standing in the doorway. She's smiling at you. You rush into your bedroom, where Neela is laying on the bed. She looks up at you and smiles, she's holding a bundled up baby in her arms. Your baby. You can't believe that this little human being is your baby. You walk over to the bed and fall to your knees next to her. You look at the bundle of joy, and your vision is starting to blur due to tears. Neela is beaming at you, she looks exhausted but happy.

"It's a boy." she says weakly.

You reach down and carefully pick up your baby boy. You hold him close to you and smell, the special baby smell. You can't believe that this baby has been in your wife's stomach for the past 9 months and now he's in your arms. You feel overwhelming joy and love towards your little baby boy. You didn't know that it was possible to feel this way, this is the happiest moment of your life.

"I love you, and I will always take care of you." you whisper to your baby.
